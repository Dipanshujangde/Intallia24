Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

What services does BrillianTech Software offer?

We offer comprehensive software development services including custom web application development, mobile app development (iOS and Android), AI-powered platform development, product development from concept to launch, quality assurance and testing services, and staff augmentation.

How long does it take to develop a custom software solution?

Development timelines vary based on project complexity, ranging from 4-6 weeks for MVP solutions to 3-6 months for enterprise applications. We follow agile methodology with regular milestones to ensure timely delivery while maintaining quality standards.

What industries do you serve?

We serve diverse industries including healthcare, finance, e-commerce, education, logistics, and SaaS startups. Our team has expertise in building solutions that meet specific industry regulations and requirements while delivering optimal user experiences.

Why choose BrillianTech Software for your project?

We combine technical expertise with business understanding, offering transparent communication, flexible engagement models, and proven methodologies. Our client-centric approach ensures your vision becomes reality with the highest quality standards.

Do you offer both project-based and staff augmentation services?

Yes, we provide flexible engagement models. Project-based for complete solutions and staff augmentation to supplement your existing teams. Both models include our quality assurance, project management, and regular progress reporting.

How do you ensure project quality and timely delivery?

We implement rigorous quality assurance processes, continuous testing, code reviews, and agile methodologies. Regular client check-ins, milestone tracking, and transparent communication ensure projects stay on schedule while meeting quality benchmarks.
