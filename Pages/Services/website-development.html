Website Development FAQ

What types of websites do you develop?

We develop all types of websites including corporate websites, e-commerce stores, web applications, portals, CMS-based websites, progressive web apps (PWAs), and custom web solutions. Our expertise spans across industries including retail, healthcare, education, finance, technology, and professional services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Website development timelines vary based on complexity. A basic business website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms or web applications can take 8-16 weeks. We follow agile methodologies with regular deliverables and provide detailed timelines during project planning.

Do you provide website hosting and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive hosting solutions with 99.9% uptime guarantee, SSL certificates, CDN, and regular backups. We also provide maintenance packages including security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support to ensure your website remains secure and up-to-date.

How do you ensure website security?

We implement multiple security layers including SSL encryption, secure coding practices, regular security updates, firewall protection, DDoS mitigation, and vulnerability scanning. We follow OWASP security guidelines and conduct regular security audits to protect against common web vulnerabilities.

Do you optimize websites for search engines (SEO)?

Yes, we build websites with SEO best practices including semantic HTML structure, optimized page speed, mobile responsiveness, proper meta tags, XML sitemaps, and clean URL structures. We also provide ongoing SEO services to improve search rankings and organic traffic.

Can you work with existing websites or redesign?

Yes, we offer website redesign and migration services. We can analyze your existing website, recommend improvements, migrate to new platforms, redesign for better user experience, and optimize for performance while preserving existing content and functionality.

What is your approach to responsive design?

We follow a mobile-first responsive design approach, ensuring websites work perfectly on all devices from smartphones to desktops. We test across multiple devices and browsers, optimize images and assets for different screen sizes, and ensure touch-friendly interfaces for mobile users.

Do you provide training and documentation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and training sessions to ensure your team can effectively manage and update the website. We believe in empowering clients with the knowledge and tools needed for long-term success.
