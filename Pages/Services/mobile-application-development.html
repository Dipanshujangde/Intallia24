Mobile App Development FAQ

What mobile platforms do you develop for?

We develop native and cross-platform mobile applications for iOS (iPhone, iPad) and Android devices. We offer native development using Swift/Kotlin, cross-platform development using React Native and Flutter, Progressive Web Apps (PWAs), and hybrid apps using modern frameworks. Our team has expertise in all major platforms and can recommend the best approach based on your requirements, budget, and timeline.

How much does it cost to develop a mobile app?

The cost of mobile app development varies depending on complexity, features, platforms, and design requirements. Simple apps can start from $10,000, while more complex enterprise solutions can range from $50,000 to $200,000+. We provide detailed project estimates after understanding your specific requirements.

How long does it take to build a mobile app?

Development timelines depend on app complexity. A basic MVP can be delivered in 2-3 months, while more sophisticated applications typically take 4-6 months. Our agile development process ensures regular deliverables and flexibility to adjust scope as needed.

Do you provide UI/UX design for mobile apps?

Yes, we have a dedicated team of UI/UX designers who create intuitive, user-friendly designs following platform-specific guidelines (Material Design for Android, Human Interface Guidelines for iOS). Our design process includes user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

How do you ensure mobile app security?

We implement multiple security layers including data encryption, secure authentication (OAuth, biometrics), secure API communication (HTTPS, SSL pinning), code obfuscation, and regular security testing. We follow OWASP Mobile Security guidelines and conduct penetration testing before launch.

Do you handle App Store and Google Play submissions?

Yes, we manage the complete app submission process including preparing store assets (screenshots, descriptions), configuring store listings, handling developer accounts, and navigating review processes. We have a 95%+ first-time approval rate due to our expertise with platform guidelines.

What happens after the app is launched?

We provide post-launch support including monitoring, bug fixes, performance optimization, and updates for new OS versions. We also offer maintenance packages for ongoing improvements, feature additions, and analytics review to ensure your app continues to meet business goals.

Can you maintain or update an existing mobile app?

Yes, we offer app maintenance and update services. We can analyze your existing codebase, suggest improvements, fix bugs, add new features, and ensure compatibility with the latest OS versions. Our team works with both native and cross-platform codebases.
